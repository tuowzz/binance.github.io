Binance vs Coinbase – Which is Better in 2025?

Looking to choose between Binance and Coinbase? Let’s compare both platforms in terms of fees, features, and user experience.

📊 Feature Comparison

  • Fees: Binance offers lower trading fees (0.1%) compared to Coinbase (~0.5%+).
  • Coins Available: Binance offers 350+ coins, while Coinbase supports ~250.
  • Beginner Friendly: Coinbase has a simpler interface, ideal for newbies.
  • Advanced Tools: Binance has futures, margin, and launchpad tools.

🌍 Global Availability

Binance serves more countries than Coinbase, especially in Asia, Africa, and Latin America.
